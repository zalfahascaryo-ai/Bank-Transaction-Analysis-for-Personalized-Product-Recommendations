## **🔥 Key Insight**
Across both clustering approaches, three consistent customer segments emerge:
* Young, low-balance users → need accessible, low-cost, digital-first banking
* Middle-aged, financially stable users → ideal for investment and wealth growth products
* Older, low-balance users → prioritize security, simplicity, and accessibility

These insights can be directly used to design personalized financial products and targeted marketing strategies.
